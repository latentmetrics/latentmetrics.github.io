Mission
“LMI investigates the mathematical structures that make physics-informed learning provably reliable — with focus on error structure, regularity conditions, and convergence behavior in Physics-Informed Neural Networks applied to complex physical systems.”

Latent Metrics Institute (LMI) is an independent research and educational organization founded and directed by Bong-Sik Kim, Ph.D., a mathematician specializing in the intersection of nonlinear partial differential equations and Scientific Machine Learning.

The Institute’s research centers on a precise question: what are the hidden mathematical structures — the latent metrics — that determine whether a Physics-Informed Neural Network faithfully respects physical law? Answering it rigorously requires tools from functional analysis, regularization theory, and numerical analysis.

On the educational side, LMI develops advanced curricula that bring mathematical depth to AI and data science. Consulting services are available for research teams and institutions seeking rigorous mathematical foundations in machine learning system design.
